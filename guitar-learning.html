Guitar Basics

ðŸŽ¸ Parts of the Guitar

  • Headstock: The top part where you tune the strings
  • Neck: The long part you hold with your left hand
  • Frets: The metal bars on the neck that divide the notes
  • Body: The big hollow part that makes the sound louder
  • Sound hole: The round hole in the body
  • Strings: The 6 wires you pluck to make music!

âœ‹ How to Hold Your Guitar

  • Sit up straight with the guitar resting on your lap
  • The guitar's body should rest on your right leg (if you're right-handed)
  • Your left hand goes on the neck to press the strings
  • Your right hand strums or picks near the sound hole
  • Keep your shoulders relaxed!

ðŸ‘† Reading Chord Diagrams

  • Vertical lines = the 6 guitar strings
  • Horizontal lines = the frets on the guitar neck
  • Colored circles with numbers = where to put your fingers
  • X = don't play this string (muted)
  • O = play this string open (no finger on it)
  • Numbers show which finger to use: 1=Index, 2=Middle, 3=Ring, 4=Pinky
